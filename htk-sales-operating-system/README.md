# HTK SALES OPERATING SYSTEM

This repository is the complete operating system for closers selling Hard To Kill Training coaching.

HTK closers do not use generic sales tactics. They run a disciplined performance-coaching sales process:

1. Confirm fit.
2. Understand the prospect's goals, obstacles, and commitment level.
3. Diagnose whether HTK coaching is the right vehicle.
4. Present the offer clearly.
5. Close qualified prospects without pressure.
6. Disqualify poor-fit prospects quickly.
7. Protect the HTK brand on every call, message, and CRM note.

## Non-Negotiable Standards

- Sell only when HTK is a legitimate fit.
- Do not position HTK as bodybuilding coaching.
- Do not make guarantees.
- Do not give medical advice.
- Do not pressure, shame, manipulate, or manufacture urgency.
- Do not quote or invent policies, pricing, refunds, or deliverables outside manager-approved current terms.
- CRM accuracy is part of the sale. If it is not logged, it did not happen.

## Operating Rule

HTK sales must be clear, professional, human, disciplined, and performance-driven. The closer's job is not to win arguments. The closer's job is to help qualified prospects make a clear decision and protect the brand when the prospect is not a fit.
