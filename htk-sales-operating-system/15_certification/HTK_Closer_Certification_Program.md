# 15. HTK Closer Certification Program

No closer handles live HTK prospects until certified.

## Certification Objective

A certified HTK closer can:

- Explain HTK positioning accurately.
- Identify A-tier, B-tier, and C-tier prospects.
- Run complete discovery.
- Diagnose fit without giving medical advice.
- Present HTK clearly.
- Handle objections without pressure.
- Close qualified prospects.
- Disqualify poor-fit prospects.
- Use CRM correctly.
- Complete enrollment and handoff.
- Protect the brand.

## Training Sequence

### Phase 1: Positioning

Closer must read:

- `01_positioning/HTK_Positioning.md`
- `02_ideal_client_profile/Ideal_Client_Profile.md`
- `14_brand_protection/HTK_Brand_Protection_Rules.md`

Certification task:

- Explain HTK in 60 seconds.
- Explain what HTK is not.
- Describe A-tier, B-tier, and C-tier prospects.
- Identify three prohibited claims.

Passing standard:

- 90%+ accuracy.
- No bodybuilding-only positioning.
- No guarantees or medical claims.

### Phase 2: Qualification and Disqualification

Closer must read:

- `03_disqualification/Disqualification_Framework.md`
- `06_needs_analysis/HTK_Needs_Analysis.md`

Certification task:

- Score 10 sample prospects.
- Mark each as sell, continue discovery, disqualify, or escalate.
- Explain each decision in one sentence.

Passing standard:

- 9 out of 10 correct.
- No unsafe prospect approved.

### Phase 3: Discovery Call Execution

Closer must read:

- `05_discovery_call/Discovery_Call_Framework.md`
- `templates/Discovery_Call_Notes.md`

Certification task:

- Run two role-play discovery calls.
- Complete discovery notes.
- Identify goal, obstacle, consequence, desired outcome, commitment, and decision process.

Passing standard:

- Uses full call structure.
- Does not pitch early.
- Asks one question at a time.
- Documents accurately.

### Phase 4: Offer Presentation

Closer must read:

- `07_offer_presentation/Offer_Presentation_Script.md`
- `09_closing/Closing_Framework.md`

Certification task:

- Present HTK offer in role play.
- Tie offer to prospect goal and obstacle.
- Explain accountability and commitment.
- Ask for a decision.

Passing standard:

- Clear and specific.
- No hype.
- No false urgency.
- No guarantees.

### Phase 5: Objection Handling

Closer must read:

- `08_objection_handling/Objection_Handling_Library.md`

Certification task:

- Handle eight standard objections:
  - I need to think about it.
  - It's too expensive.
  - I don't have time.
  - I can do this myself.
  - Maybe later.
  - I need to talk to my spouse.
  - I want to try free content first.
  - I'm not sure it'll work for me.

Passing standard:

- Clarifies real objection.
- Does not argue.
- Knows when to stop.
- Keeps brand voice.

### Phase 6: CRM and Enrollment

Closer must read:

- `10_payment_enrollment/Payment_Enrollment_Process.md`
- `11_crm/CRM_Requirements.md`
- `templates/Enrollment_Handoff_Template.md`

Certification task:

- Complete three sample CRM records.
- Process a mock enrollment.
- Submit an onboarding handoff.

Passing standard:

- 98%+ required fields complete.
- Payment not marked won before confirmation.
- Handoff includes goal, obstacle, context, and risk notes.

## Final Certification Exam

Closer must complete:

1. Written positioning test.
2. Prospect fit test.
3. Live role-play discovery call.
4. Live role-play offer and close.
5. Objection handling drill.
6. CRM record review.
7. Enrollment handoff review.

Passing score:

## Live Call Ramp

After certification:

### First 5 Calls

- Manager reviews every call.
- Closer may not change approved pricing/terms.
- CRM reviewed same day.

### Calls 6-20

- Manager samples 50%.
- Objection handling reviewed weekly.
- Follow-up discipline checked daily.

### After 20 Calls

- Standard QA cadence applies.
- Any compliance issue returns closer to supervised status.
