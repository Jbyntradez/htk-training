# 13. Sales KPIs

Sales KPIs measure volume, quality, conversion, revenue, and brand safety.

## Daily KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Daily Target |
|---|---|---:|
| Calls booked | New qualified calls scheduled | 2-5 |
| Calls completed | Discovery calls actually completed | 2-4 |
| Show rate | Completed calls divided by scheduled calls due | 70%+ |
| Offers presented | Qualified calls receiving offer | 1-3 |
| New clients enrolled | Paid enrollments | 0-2 |
| Revenue collected | Confirmed paid revenue | Track daily |
| Follow-ups sent | Due follow-ups completed | 100% of due |
| CRM completion | Completed required fields | 98%+ |
| Brand/compliance incidents | Confirmed violations | 0 |

## Weekly KPIs

| KPI | Healthy Standard |
|---|---:|
| Calls booked | 10-25 |
| Calls completed | 8-20 |
| Show rate | 70%+ |
| Offer rate | 50%-80% of completed calls |
| Close rate from offers | 25%-50% |
| Close rate from completed calls | 15%-35% |
| Revenue collected | Manager-defined target |
| Follow-up conversion | 10%-25% of follow-up prospects enroll or rebook |
| CRM completion | 98%+ |
| Refund/complaint flags from sales expectations | 0 |

## Quality Metrics

### Discovery Quality

Sampled calls must show:

- Clear goal identified.
- Current situation understood.
- Main obstacle clarified.
- Consequence of inaction discussed.
- Commitment level tested.
- Fit/disqualification decision documented.

Target: 90%+ pass.

### Offer Quality

Sampled offers must:

- Tie to prospect's goal.
- Explain HTK accurately.
- Set commitment expectations.
- Avoid guarantees.
- Avoid hype and pressure.
- Present current terms accurately.

Target: 95%+ pass.

### CRM Quality

Sampled records must include:

- Stage
- Goal
- Obstacle
- Outcome
- Next action/date or final reason
- Payment status if won
- Handoff if enrolled

Target: 98%+ complete.

## Diagnosis Guide

| Pattern | Likely Issue | Management Action |
|---|---|---|
| Low show rate | Weak confirmation or low-intent booking | Audit booking and reminders |
| High calls, low offers | Poor fit or weak discovery | Audit qualification |
| High offers, low closes | Offer presentation or objection handling issue | Review call recordings |
| High closes, high complaints | Expectations oversold | Review brand compliance |
| High follow-up volume, low conversion | Weak decision process | Coach closing clarity |
| Low CRM completion | Rushed process | Reduce volume until quality recovers |
