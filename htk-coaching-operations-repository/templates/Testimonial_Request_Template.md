# HTK Testimonial Request Template

## Approval Checklist

- [ ] Client has meaningful progress.
- [ ] Client has no unresolved complaint.
- [ ] Claim is accurate.
- [ ] Consent will be documented.
- [ ] No medical or guaranteed-result claim.

## Request Message

> You have made real progress with [specific result]. If you are open to it, HTK may want to share your story to help other people understand what the coaching process looks like. No pressure either way. If you are open, I can send a few prompts and we will only use what you approve.

## Prompts

- What was your main goal when you started?
- What was not working before HTK?
- What changed during coaching?
- What result are you most proud of?
- What would you tell someone considering HTK?
