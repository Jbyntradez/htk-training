# HTK Coaching Operations Repository

This repository is the operating system for delivering Hard To Kill Training coaching at scale.

The goal is simple: a newly hired coach should be able to deliver the HTK experience consistently without Jimmy Bynum personally coaching every client.

HTK coaching is performance coaching. It is not bodybuilding coaching, medical treatment, injury rehabilitation, physical therapy, or generic fitness advice.

## Operating Standard

Every coach must protect four things:

1. Client safety.
2. HTK performance standard.
3. Client accountability.
4. Brand reputation.

## Non-Negotiables

- Do not diagnose, treat, or rehab injuries.
- Do not give medical advice.
- Do not promise outcomes.
- Do not position HTK as bodybuilding coaching.
- Do not ignore missed check-ins, non-compliance, or safety flags.
- Do not change programming without a reason tied to the client's goal, data, readiness, or constraints.
- Document every meaningful coaching action in the CRM.

## Coach Operating Rule

Coach the client in front of you, but deliver the HTK system consistently. The coach adapts execution; the coach does not reinvent the brand.
