# Scope Practice References

These references inform the conservative HTK scope-of-practice and injury-escalation rules.

Coaches must still follow HTK policy, manager direction, and applicable local laws.

## References

- ACE personal trainer role and scope of practice manual excerpt: https://www.acefitness.org/academy/AcademyElitePDFs/ACE_PT5th_Manual_Ch1.pdf
- ACE nutrition scope of practice article: https://www.acefitness.org/resources/pros/expert-articles/6248/nutrition-scope-of-practice-what-you-can-do-as-a-personal-trainer/
- NSCA Certified Performance and Sport Scientist scope of practice: https://www.nsca.com/certification/cpss/scope-of-practice/
- NSCA personal trainers professional area: https://www.nsca.com/professional-development/professional-areas/personal-trainers/
- ACSM Preparticipation Physical Evaluation Monograph page: https://acsm.org/education-resources/books/preparticipation-physical-evaluation-monograph/
- ACSM Exercise is Medicine credential page: https://acsm.org/certification/specialized/acsm-exercise-is-medicine-credential/

## HTK Application

HTK coaches train, coach, adjust, document, and refer out. HTK coaches do not diagnose, treat, prescribe rehab, or provide medical advice.
