# VA Performance Standards

## Standard Of Work

The HTK outreach VA is measured on qualified conversations and brand-safe execution, not raw activity alone.

A strong VA:

- Selects relevant prospects.
- Writes short contextual messages.
- Responds promptly.
- Qualifies without pressure.
- Schedules the right calls.
- Maintains a clean CRM.
- Escalates risk early.

## Daily KPI Targets

| KPI | Definition | Target | Cap |
| --- | --- | ---: | ---: |
| New personalized DMs sent | First-contact manual DMs sent to researched prospects | 20 | 25 |
| Follow-ups sent | Manual follow-up DMs sent according to the follow-up schedule | 15 | 20 |
| Conversations started | Prospects who send a meaningful reply after outreach | 6 | None |
| Qualified leads | Prospects moved to `Qualified` after meeting every required condition | 2 | None |
| Calls scheduled | Qualified prospects with a confirmed date, time, time zone, and calendar email | 1 | None |

## Weekly Performance Benchmarks

Based on a five-day production week:

| KPI | Healthy range |
| --- | ---: |
| New personalized DMs | 90-110 |
| Follow-ups | 55-80 |
| Conversations started | 22-35 |
| Qualified leads | 7-12 |
| Calls scheduled | 3-6 |
| CRM completeness | 98% or higher |
| Compliance violations | 0 |

## Quality Standards

### Prospect Quality

At least 85% of sampled first-contact prospects must clearly fit the HTK target market.

### Message Quality

At least 90% of sampled first-contact DMs must:

- Reference a real profile signal or relevant context.
- Ask one easy-to-answer question.
- Sound human.
- Avoid a first-message link.
- Avoid claims, pressure, and hype.

### CRM Quality

At least 98% of sampled records must include:

- X handle
- Profile URL
- Prospect segment
- Public signal
- Current stage
- Last contact date
- Next action
- Next action date

### Response Time

During the VA's assigned shift:

- Reply to active prospect messages within 60 minutes.
- Escalate brand-risk messages within 15 minutes.
- Record scheduled-call details immediately.

## Performance Levels

| Level | Standard |
| --- | --- |
| Excellent | Hits quality standards, maintains complete CRM records, averages 4 or more scheduled calls per week, and has zero compliance issues |
| Meets Standard | Hits quality standards, maintains at least 98% CRM completeness, averages 3 or more scheduled calls per week, and has zero compliance issues |
| Needs Coaching | Misses one quality standard, averages fewer than 3 scheduled calls per week for two weeks, or allows CRM completeness below 98% |
| Immediate Review | Uses automation, sends duplicate bulk DMs, ignores an opt-out, misrepresents HTK, shares private information, or creates an account-security risk |

## Corrective Action Process

1. Identify the failed standard using CRM records and message samples.
2. Review 10 examples with the VA.
3. Define one behavior to correct during the next three workdays.
4. Reduce outreach volume if quality is the issue.
5. Re-score after three workdays.
6. Escalate to the manager if the same issue remains.

Compliance violations trigger immediate review. Volume never excuses unsafe behavior.
