# Qualification Framework

## Purpose

The VA qualifies whether a conversation is worth moving to Jimmy. The VA does not conduct a sales call inside X DMs.

Qualification should feel like a useful conversation, not an interview.

## The Five-Part Qualification Gate

A lead becomes `Qualified` only when all five conditions are confirmed:

| Gate | What must be true | Example evidence |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1. Market fit | Prospect belongs to an HTK segment | Former football player, veteran, first responder, athlete, or performance-focused lifter |
| 2. Performance goal | Prospect wants a performance outcome | Wants better conditioning, mobility, explosiveness, durability, or athleticism |
| 3. Meaningful obstacle | Prospect has a real gap or constraint | Inconsistent plan, stiffness, poor conditioning, schedule, or training without progress |
| 4. Coaching openness | Prospect is open to structured support | Says coaching, accountability, or a plan could help |
| 5. Call readiness | Prospect is willing to speak with Jimmy within 14 days | Agrees to a call and provides scheduling details |

Do not move a prospect to `Qualified` because he replied, was friendly, or likes fitness.

## Conversation Sequence

Do not send all questions at once. Ask only the next unanswered question.

### Question 1: Current Training

Use:

> What are you training for right now?

Alternative:

> What does your training look like these days?

### Question 2: Desired Outcome

Use:

> If you could improve one thing over the next few months, what would make the biggest difference: strength, conditioning, mobility, speed, or just feeling athletic again?

### Question 3: Main Obstacle

Use:

> What has been the main thing holding that back?

Alternative:

> Where do you feel the biggest gap right now?

### Question 4: Urgency

Use:

> Is that something you want to fix now, or are you mostly exploring ideas for later?

### Question 5: Coaching Openness

Use:

> Would you be open to a structured coaching plan if it matched what you are trying to build?

### Transition To Booking

Use only when the five-part gate is satisfied:

> Based on what you said, it sounds like a conversation with Jimmy would be useful. He can look at where you are now, what you are training for, and whether HTK is a fit. No pressure either way. Want me to set that up?

## Qualification Score

Use the score for reporting and prioritization:

| Category | 0 points | 1 point | 2 points |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Market fit | No clear fit | General fitness interest | Clear priority segment |
| Goal clarity | No goal | General improvement | Specific performance outcome |
| Obstacle | No gap stated | Mild frustration | Clear obstacle or cost of staying stuck |
| Urgency | Exploring later | Wants progress eventually | Wants action within 90 days |
| Coaching openness | Not interested | Unsure | Open to structured support |

Interpretation:

| Score | Action |
| ---: | --- |
| 8-10 | Invite to a call when all required gates are confirmed |
| 5-7 | Continue the conversation and clarify the missing gate |
| 0-4 | Do not push for a call; close respectfully or keep as a long-term relationship only when appropriate |

The score supports judgment. It does not override the five required gates.

## Disqualification Rules

Mark `Closed Lost` and record the reason when:

- Prospect says no or asks not to receive messages.
- Prospect wants bodybuilding-only or appearance-only coaching and rejects performance training.
- Prospect is under 18.
- Prospect wants free coaching in DMs and has no interest in a call.
- Prospect is not open to coaching.
- Prospect cannot or will not speak within the next 14 days after a clear invitation.
- Prospect is abusive, explicit, or clearly unsafe.
- Prospect is outside the target market and there is no clear HTK fit.

## Long-Term Relationship Rule

If the prospect is a fit but timing is not right:

1. Do not keep following up repeatedly.
2. Ask once:

> Understood. Want me to leave it there for now?

3. If the prospect says yes, mark `Closed Lost` with reason `Timing - prospect asked to pause`.
4. Do not contact again unless the prospect re-engages or the manager approves a specific relationship-based message.

## Sensitive Information

Do not ask for:

- Medical diagnosis
- Injury details beyond what the prospect voluntarily states
- Credit-card information
- Passwords
- Government ID
- Home address
- Income

For booking, collect only name, calendar email, time zone, and two available time windows.
