# Booking Process

## Objective

Schedule qualified prospects with Jimmy using a reliable manual workflow.

The VA invites the call only after the prospect meets the qualification gate. The VA does not push a call on every respondent.

## When To Invite A Call

Invite a call only when the prospect:

- Fits the HTK market.
- Has a performance goal.
- Has a meaningful obstacle.
- Wants to make progress now.
- Is open to coaching support.

## Call Invitation

Send:

> Based on what you said, it sounds like a conversation with Jimmy would be useful. He can look at where you are now, what you are training for, and whether HTK is a fit. No pressure either way. Want me to set that up?

## If The Prospect Says Yes

Send:

> Good. Send your time zone, the best email for the calendar invite, and two windows that work for a 30-minute call over the next seven days.

Collect only:

- Prospect name
- Calendar email
- Time zone
- Two available 30-minute windows within the next seven days

Do not ask for medical information, payment information, or a home address.

## Manual Scheduling Workflow

1. Confirm the prospect is marked `Qualified`.
2. Ask for the prospect's name, calendar email, time zone, and two windows.
3. Review Jimmy's approved availability calendar.
4. Choose an available 30-minute slot that matches one of the requested windows.
5. Create the calendar event titled `HTK Coaching Fit Call - Prospect Name`.
6. Add Jimmy Bynum and the prospect as attendees.
7. Add this event description:

> HTK coaching fit call with Jimmy Bynum. We will discuss your current training, your performance goal, what has been holding progress back, and whether HTK coaching is the right next step.

8. Send the calendar invitation.
9. Send the confirmation DM:

> You are set for the HTK coaching fit call with Jimmy. I sent the calendar invite to your email. Please accept it when you see it. If anything changes, message us here as soon as possible.

10. Record the confirmed date, time, time zone, and calendar email in the CRM.
11. Move the lead to `Call Scheduled`.
12. Add the call to the manager handoff list.

## If Neither Window Is Available

Send:

> Those windows are booked. Would either of these work for a 30-minute call: Tuesday at 2:00 PM Pacific or Thursday at 11:00 AM Pacific?

Use two real open times from Jimmy's approved availability calendar. Always include the time zone.

## Confirmation And Reminder Workflow

### Immediately After Scheduling

Send the confirmation DM and confirm the CRM record is complete.

### 24 Hours Before The Call

Send:

> Quick reminder that your HTK coaching fit call with Jimmy is tomorrow at the time on your calendar invite. If anything changed, message us here as soon as possible.

### Two Hours Before The Call

Send:

> Your HTK call with Jimmy is coming up in about two hours. Talk soon.

## Rescheduling

If the prospect needs to reschedule:

> No problem. Send two windows that work over the next seven days and your time zone, and I will move the invite.

Update the calendar event and CRM immediately.

## No-Show Workflow

If the prospect misses the call:

1. Mark the CRM note `No-show`.
2. Send:

> Looks like the call did not connect today. If you still want to speak with Jimmy, send two windows that work over the next seven days and I will help reset it.

3. Send one final follow-up three calendar days later if needed:

> Closing the loop on the missed call. If you want to revisit HTK later, message us and we will pick it back up.

4. If there is no reply, move to `Closed Lost` with reason `No-show - no reschedule`.

## Manager Handoff

For every scheduled call, send the manager:

- Prospect name
- X handle
- Call date and time with time zone
- Calendar email
- Segment
- Performance goal
- Main obstacle
- Why now
- Qualification score
- Any relevant conversation note
