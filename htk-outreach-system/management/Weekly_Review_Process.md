# Weekly Review Process

## Objective

Use a 30-minute weekly review to improve booked-call quality while protecting the HTK brand.

The manager runs the review after the final workday of each week.

## Inputs

Collect:

- Five daily reports
- Completed `templates/KPI_Dashboard.csv`
- Lead tracker
- 10 randomly selected first-contact DMs
- Five active or completed qualification conversations
- Every scheduled-call handoff
- Closed-lost reasons
- X account warnings, restrictions, or negative feedback

## Weekly Review Agenda

### 1. Compliance Gate: 5 Minutes

Check:

- Were all messages sent manually?
- Did the VA use only the authorized account?
- Were opt-outs honored immediately?
- Did any first-contact message contain a link?
- Were duplicate messages sent repeatedly?
- Did X display any warning or DM limitation?
- Did the VA represent HTK accurately?

If any serious issue appears, stop outreach and perform an immediate review before discussing volume.

### 2. KPI Review: 5 Minutes

Compare actual weekly results with:

| KPI | Weekly target |
| --- | ---: |
| New personalized DMs | 100 |
| Follow-ups | 75 |
| Conversations started | 30 |
| Qualified leads | 10 |
| Calls scheduled | 5 |

Calculate:

- Response rate
- Qualification rate
- Booking rate from qualified leads
- Booking rate from new DMs
- Show rate

### 3. Message QA: 8 Minutes

Score 10 sampled first-contact DMs:

- Was the prospect an HTK fit?
- Did the message use a real public signal?
- Was it short?
- Did it ask one easy-to-answer question?
- Did it avoid a link, hype, and pressure?
- Did it sound distinct from the other messages?

Target: at least 9 of 10 pass.

### 4. CRM QA: 5 Minutes

Score 10 sampled lead records:

- Correct X handle and URL
- Correct stage
- Segment recorded
- Public signal recorded
- Last contact date recorded
- Next action and date recorded
- Opt-out status recorded when relevant
- Booking details complete when relevant

Target: at least 98% of required fields complete.

### 5. Pipeline Review: 5 Minutes

Review:

- Every `Qualified` lead without a scheduled call
- Every upcoming scheduled call
- Every no-show
- Every active conversation without a next action
- Top three closed-lost reasons

### 6. One Improvement: 2 Minutes

Choose one behavior for the next week.

Examples:

- Improve former-athlete prospect selection.
- Ask the obstacle question earlier.
- Shorten first-contact messages.
- Send scheduling details faster.
- Improve calendar reminder discipline.

Do not assign more than one core improvement at a time.

## Weekly Decision Rules

| Result | Action |
| --- | --- |
| Quality standards met and 3 or more calls scheduled | Continue standard targets |
| Quality standards met and fewer than 3 calls scheduled | Keep volume steady and improve the weakest conversion point |
| Message QA below 90% | Reduce new-DM target to 15 per day and retrain scripts |
| CRM completeness below 98% | Reduce new-DM target until records are corrected |
| Show rate below 70% | Audit reminders and booking confirmation |
| Any compliance violation | Stop outreach and perform immediate corrective review |

Complete `templates/Weekly_Review_Template.md` after the meeting and store it with the week's reports.
